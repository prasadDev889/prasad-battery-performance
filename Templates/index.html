About BatteryOptima

BatteryOptima is an advanced analytics platform designed to solve one of the biggest challenges in the EV industry: battery longevity and efficiency. Our system utilizes state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms like XGBoost and LightGBM to predict State of Health (SOH) with unparalleled accuracy.

Core Capabilities

SOH Prediction

Real-time estimation of battery State of Health based on voltage, current, and temperature.

Advanced ML

Leveraging LightGBM and XGBoost for high-precision predictive modeling.

Safety First

Early warning systems for temperature fluctuations and energy consumption spikes.
